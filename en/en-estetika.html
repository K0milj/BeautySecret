SHR - IPL TREATMENTS

The device has several functions:

  • EPILATION
  • PODMLADjIVANjE KOŽE (UKLANjANjE BORA)
  • SKIN REJUVENATION (WRINKLE REMOVAL)
  • HYPERPIGMENTATION THERAPY (STAIN REMOVAL)
  • ACNE THERAPY
  • BREAST LIFTING

For each of the mentioned treatments, it is necessary to put an appropriate filter in the probe, which determines the wavelength of the light beam, which is needed to treat a certain problem.

EPILATION

SHR (Super Hair Removal) - IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) device works on the principle of heating the dermis to a temperature that effectively destroys hair follicles and prevents their regrowth, while not damaging the surrounding tissue. Almost without any pain, the impulses, which are repeated at high speed and average strength, penetrate deep into the dermis and generate heat.

The applicator is constantly moving over the entire area to be treated, and at the same time the ICETM technique cools the skin surface, which prevents the appearance of burns and pain, and yet achieves effective hair removal.

SHR slowly heats the skin, until the follicle receives the necessary heat, which makes this method the safest method of laser hair removal for all skin types, including dark skin. This claim has been proven in many clinical studies.

This method can also be practiced on people whose skin has darkened from exposure to the sun's rays, and can therefore be applied throughout the year.

During the treatment, the client feels only heat on the skin, possibly a slight tingling. After one series of treatments that are done for 3-4 weeks (due to the stages of hair growth), the "maintenance" of the obtained result is not necessary.

DEPILATION

Depilation is the process of removing unwanted hair in the finest way. The eternal fight against unwanted hair has become a part of everyday care. Depilation achieves the effect of soft and smooth skin, and at the same time the necessary peeling is obtained, which removes dead skin layers.

WAXING WITH WAX

Hot wax depilation is the most common type of depilation. In this way, the shortest and thinnest hairs are removed very successfully. The wax is heated to about 60 to 70 degrees and applied to the skin in the direction of hair growth, and then the cooled one is removed from the skin in quick strokes. This way, the hairs are perfectly removed because the wax pulls them out of the roots. Hot wax depilation is an ideal solution for lasting results that successfully eliminate the problem of ingrown small subcutaneous hairs.

COLD WAX DEPILATION

Cold waxing is a quick and very effective way to get rid of hair quickly. If you remove the small ones in this way, you will be calm for at least two to three weeks.
The procedure of removing unwanted hair by treating with cold wax depilation is done by applying a thin layer of wax in the direction of hair growth, and then the cosmetic depilatory tape that sticks to the applied layer of wax is torn in the opposite direction from hair growth. In this way, the hair is pulled from the roots, and the skin becomes beautiful and smooth after a short time.
