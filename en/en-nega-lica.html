NEEDLE-FREE MESOTHERAPY

Mesotherapy is a modern, non-invasive, clinically tested and safe method for face and body care. The mesoporation method enables faster and deeper penetration of active substances, which activates cell metabolism. This method is based on the action of a pulse current that allows vitamin and mineral cocktails to penetrate the dermis, where collagen and elastic fibers are synthesized. Cocktails contain active substances necessary for skin regeneration and revitalization. The effects achieved with this treatment are reduction of wrinkles, raising facial tone, hydration of the skin, removal of dark circles under the eyes, as well as other pigmentation on the face, neck and neck line. Mesoporation can also affect parts of the body where cellulite is present. The cocktail has a lipolytic and diuretic effect, which eliminates fat deposits and solves the problem of fluid retention. Herbal extracts and vitamins contained in the cocktail act on the skin, which becomes firmer and tighter. This method is safe and painless, without any side effects after treatment. The treatments are done once a week, and for the full effect, it is necessary to do a series of 6 treatments. After the series, in order to maintain the effect, it is enough to do one treatment every two months.

RADIO WAVE LIFTING

RTL is an effective, non-surgical treatment for rejuvenating the skin of the face and body. Radio waves act on the dermis, where the heat of the radio waves stimulates elastic fibers and arouses their natural synthesis, which tightens the skin and makes it radiant and shiny. RTL alleviates the existing signs of aging, but also acts preventively, in order to delay the appearance of these signs. Face and body, wrinkles and lines around the eyes, lowered eyelids, bags under the eyes, wrinkles on the forehead and face, relaxed cheeks, nasolabial and neck wrinkles and chin can be treated. Also, relaxed skin of the neck line, chest, arms, abdomen, thighs, knees is treated. The treatments are painless, with no side effects. The long-lasting effect is achieved with a series of 3-6 treatments, with an interval of 7-14 days. One treatment every two months is recommended to maintain the effect

DERMAPEN

Dermapen can be classified as a mesotherapy method. This method is far more advanced than of the original techniques of mesoaesthetics. It is one of the best treatments of today, with highly effective results.
Dermapen is a device that effectively stimulates collagen synthesis and enables better absorption of cosmetic preparations. The disposable insert on the top of the brace contains a number of needles that stimulate tissue regeneration and inject active substances at the same time. The advantage of this device is that its needles are 5 times thinner and 100 times thicker than others similar apparatus. It is also smaller, which makes it easier to access smaller regions (around the eyes, nose). The treatment is less painful, but also includes the possibility of using a local anesthetic. Its basic advantage is that different skin problems and conditions can be treated during one treatment, because the needles are adjustable from 0.2-2.5mm.
By creating programmed and controlled microcracks on the skin, the skin is stimulated to react naturally to the regeneration and creation of new elastic and collagen fibers. Depending on the length of the needles, dermapen can achieve more results - from surface hydration, improved penetration of cosmetics, to the treatment and nourshment of more serious problems (acne scars, damage to skin, deep wrinkles, alopecia, etc.).
Indications are numerous: the need to improve the texture and tone of the skin, superficial wrinkles, deep wrinkles, resorption of cosmetics, stretch marks, scars, alopecia, pigmentation, enlarged pores, acne. The treatment is performed on cleansed skin. Different cocktails are added depending on the indication. The goal of the treatment is to reach the subcutaneous tissue, because this is where the problems usually arouse. In this way, they are solved permanently, because it is not a matter of surface treatment that gives short-term results. The results achieved by dermapen are long-lasting. Depending on the goal of the treatment, the results are visible after two weeks (surface treatments: hydration, stain removal…) or in 2-4 months (treatment of scars and other damage, where the results are visible immediately, but a lasting effect is possible only after 2 months when new collagen is formed).
The treatments are done for a month, and it is necessary to do 3-5 repetitions for extremely visible results. Maintenance is once every 3-6 months.
After the treatment, the skin is visibly red and slightly inflamed, so you should avoid exposure to strong sunlight, physical activity, the use of peels and strong preparations 3-4 days after the treatment.
